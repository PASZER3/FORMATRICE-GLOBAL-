What this estimate doesn't capture

Assumes uniform wall thickness, a single flat cavity plane, and isothermal flow. It doesn't model ribs, thickness transitions, true 3D melt-front cooling, packing/shrinkage, or warpage. Use it to screen gate placement and flag obvious problem zones — confirm anything critical in real CAE software (Moldex3D, Moldflow) before cutting steel.
